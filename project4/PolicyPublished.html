Privacy Policy for Change Request Management

Last updated May 08,2023

Change Request Management ("CR Management", "we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our customers and users of our website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information, including personally identifiable information ("PII"), that we receive from you or third parties through our website and other services.At Change Request Management, we respect and protect the privacy of our users.This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and share personally identifiable information (PII), as well as our cookie policy and third-party information usage.

Collection of Personally Identifiable Information (PII)

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and address when you interact with our website or submit a change request. We use this information to fulfill your requests, improve our services, and communicate with you about our products and services.

Usage of Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services and to fulfill your requests. We may use your personal information to contact you about our products and services, including marketing communications, unless you opt-out. We may share your personal information with trusted third-party service providers to help us provide, maintain, and improve our services. These third-party service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and may not use your information for any other purpose.

Cookie Policy

We use cookies and other tracking technologies to improve your experience on our website, to personalize content and ads, and to analyze our website traffic. Cookies are small data files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, but please note that some features of our website may not function properly without cookies.

Third Party Information Usage

We may use third-party services such as Google Analytics to collect and analyze website usage data. These services may use cookies and other tracking technologies to collect information about your use of our website, including your IP address, browser type, and other information. We may also include links to third-party websites on our website. These third-party websites have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them before providing any personal information.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at CR@CR.org or call us at 1800-000-000. You can also write to us at our physical address: 9000 Green Moon, Takoma Park, MD 21075.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time without prior notice. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your privacy.

Data Rotation for One Year

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide you with our services and comply with our legal obligations. We may also retain your information for a reasonable period of time to allow us to analyze usage patterns, improve our services, or comply with legal obligations. After one year, we will delete your PII and other information from our systems, unless we are required by law to retain it for a longer period of time. We're thrilled to be able to offer these new features and updates to our platform, and we hope that they will make it even easier for you to manage change requests and improve your change management processes.
